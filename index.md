# Privacy Policy

**Loop Killer** respects your privacy.

## Data We Collect
Loop Killer collects minimal, anonymous usage data to help improve the app.  
This may include:
- App usage events (e.g. feature usage, errors)
- Aggregated statistics (e.g. weekly completion rates)

No personally identifiable information is collected.

## Analytics
Analytics are:
- Anonymous
- Used only to improve app reliability and usability
- Never sold or shared with third parties for advertising

Analytics collection is optional and can be disabled in the app.

## Local Data
All personal usage data (loops, interrupts, daily entries) is stored locally on your device.  
Loop Killer does not upload or sync this data unless explicitly enabled by the user.

## Notifications
Notifications are optional and can be enabled or disabled at any time via system settings.

## Data Retention
Anonymous analytics data may be retained in aggregate form for trend analysis.  
No individual behavior timelines are stored on servers.
