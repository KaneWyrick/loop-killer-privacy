Privacy Policy

Loop Killer respects your privacy.

Data We Collect

Loop Killer collects minimal, anonymous usage data to help improve the app. This may include:

  • App usage events (e.g. feature usage, errors)
  • Aggregated statistics (e.g. weekly completion rates)

No personally identifiable information is collected.

Analytics

Analytics are anonymous, used only to improve reliability and usability, and are never sold or used for advertising.

Local Data

All personal usage data (loops, interrupts, daily entries) is stored locally on your device.
